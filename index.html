Transforming Public Transport in Addis Ababa

Innovative Solutions for Efficient Commuting

Our Mission

Addis Ababa’s public transportation system suffers from overcrowding, unreliable services, and inconsistent fares, significantly affecting commuters’ daily lives.

Our venture aims to develop a comprehensive public transport management app that enhances service reliability, provides real-time updates, and offers cashless payment options.
