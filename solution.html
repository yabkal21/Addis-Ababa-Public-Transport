Our Solution

We are developing a mobile app that streamlines public transport usage.

How It Works

Users can find nearby bus stops, track vehicle locations in real-time, and pay fares digitally.

Top Features

  • Real-time tracking
  • Cashless payments
  • User-friendly interface

Target Beneficiaries

Daily commuters, students, and low-income earners.

Implementation Plans

The app will be launched in phases, starting with a pilot program in high-density areas.

Unique Selling Points

Our app integrates all transport modes, providing a one-stop solution for commuters.
