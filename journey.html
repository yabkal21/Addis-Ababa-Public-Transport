Entrepreneurial Journey

Overview

Starting this venture, I identified the pressing issues faced by commuters in Addis Ababa. My research highlighted the need for improved transportation solutions.

Problem Statement

The lack of reliable public transport options forces commuters to waste time and money, leading to frustration and decreased productivity.

Pain Points

Commuters experience overcrowded buses, long wait times, and financial strain from fluctuating fares.

Evidence

According to a 2023 survey, 60% of commuters reported dissatisfaction with current transport services, with many spending over 15% of their income on fares.
